‎‎-Plot-

Long Way Down is a book about 15-year-old Will who lives in an unsafe part of town, where passby shootings can happen often.
One day, Will and his friend Tony were talking to each other when they started hearing gunshots, so they hid and waited it out. Once the gunshots stopped, they got up and saw that Will's brother, Shawn, was killed.
Will must now follow The Rules, so he has to kill the person whom he thinks had killed his brother.
Will finds his brother's gun and on the next day he sneaks outside and enters the elevator to head to his brother's killer's location. On the elevator, he meets the ghosts of all the people that have passed away in his life, and they make him question what he's going to do...
