‎‎-About the Author-

Jason Reynolds was born in Washington, D.C. on December 6, 1983. He has been interested in rap and poetry since he was nine years old. He wrote Long Way Down in 2017, which was given the title of a Newbery Honor book, a Printz Honor book, and the Best young adult work by the Mystery Writers of America's Edgar Awards.
